Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Yonder is built around a simple principle: your data stays on your device. This policy explains what that means.

What we collect

Nothing leaves your phone. Yonder does not have user accounts, servers, or a cloud backend. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

Location

Yonder uses your device location solely to notify you when you arrive near a place you have saved. This works through iOS region monitoring (geofencing) and significant-location-change events handled entirely by the operating system on your device.

  • Your location is never sent to us or to any third party.
  • Yonder does not continuously track, log, or record your movements.
  • The app only reacts when iOS reports that you have entered a region you chose.
  • "Always" location access is required so reminders can fire even when the app is closed or in the background. Without it, the app cannot perform its core function.

The places you save

The places, notes, and reminder settings you create are stored locally on your device only. They are not uploaded anywhere.

Notifications

Yonder uses local notifications only. These are scheduled and delivered on your device. No push notification servers are involved.

Third parties

Yonder uses Apple's MapKit to display maps and search for places, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children

Yonder does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
