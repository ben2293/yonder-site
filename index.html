Common questions

Why does Yonder need "Always" location?
Yonder's whole purpose is to notify you the moment you arrive near a place you saved. To do that even when the app is closed, iOS needs background ("Always") location access. Yonder does not track or record where you go — it only reacts when you enter a place you chose.

Is my location data sent anywhere?
No. Everything stays on your phone. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.

Why am I not getting notifications?
Make sure location permission is set to "Always" and notifications are enabled for Yonder in iOS Settings.
